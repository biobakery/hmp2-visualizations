Your Gut Similarity Compared to Others

This diagram shows all stool samples collected from all participants in the IBD Multi'omics project. Each dot represents a single sample, and the distance between points indicates how dissimilar they are. The closer together two samples' points appear, the more similar their microbial communities are. Your samples as collected over time are marked in red, so you can see how your own gut microbiome changes over time and in relation to other participants' guts.
